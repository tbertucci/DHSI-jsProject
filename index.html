Lo Questionario Digitale de Fonètica Històrica

Towards a Digital Phonetic Database of Romance

The Questionario Digitale de Fonètica Històrica (QDFH) is in its early stages as an enhanced digital edition of an index to the Atlas linguistique roman (ALiR), the Questionnaire de Phonétique Historique. The intention is essentially to be a mapped database of the Romance phonetic outcomes of Latin; for each Latin phonetic context, I've been collecting over 30 language varieties' resulting phonemes which will eventually display within each language variety's bubble.
