Lo Questionario Digitale de Fonètica Històrica

Towards a Digital Phonetic Database of Romance

The Questionario Digitale de Fonètica Històrica (QDFH), in its early stages, will become an enhanced digital edition of an index to the Atlas linguistique roman (ALiR), its Questionnaire de Phonétique Historique. The goal is essentially to be a mapped database of the Romance phonetic outcomes of Latin; for each of over 280 consonant and vowel treatments in Latin, I've been collecting the resulting phonemes for about 50 language varieties. These will eventually display within each bubble after the user selects a given treatment from a dropdown box.
